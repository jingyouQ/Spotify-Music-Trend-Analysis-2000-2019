# Spotify Music Trend Analysis (1998–2019)

## Overview
Analysis of Spotify music trends from 1998 to 2019,
focusing on popular artists, genre trends, and music style changes.

## Key Findings
- Pop and Hip Hop dominate the music market, with Pop maintaining the top position throughout the entire period
- Rock and R&B are gradually declining, being replaced by Pop and Hip Hop
- Music is becoming more emotionally negative (valence decreasing) but more danceable over time, reflecting a shift in listener preferences toward emotional resonance rather than high energy

## Tools Used
- Python
- pandas
- matplotlib
- seaborn
